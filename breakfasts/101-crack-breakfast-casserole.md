## Starbucks-Style Egg Bites
Bad Batch Baking

Servings: 6–8 egg bites | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~40 min

Ingredients
- 6 large eggs
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese (see options below)
- 1/4 cup milk or heavy cream
- 1/2 cup cooked bacon, chopped
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

Protein Options
- Cooked bacon (classic)
- Cooked breakfast sausage, crumbled
- Diced ham

Cheese Options
- Cheddar
- Monterey Jack
- Gruyere
- Swiss
- Pepper Jack

Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
2. Grease a muffin tin or silicone egg bite mold.
3. In a blender, combine eggs, cottage cheese, milk or cream, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
4. Stir in shredded cheese and chosen protein.
5. Pour mixture evenly into prepared molds.
6. Place muffin tin in a larger baking dish and fill the outer dish with hot water halfway up the sides (water bath).
7. Bake for 25–30 minutes, until centers are just set.
8. Cool slightly before removing from molds.

Notes
- Blending the eggs creates the smooth Starbucks-style texture.
- Use different protein and cheese combinations to change flavor profiles.
- Store in refrigerator up to 4 days.
- Reheat gently in microwave.
