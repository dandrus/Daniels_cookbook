## 102. Starbucks-Style Egg Bites

===============================================
Breakfast   |   Eggs   |   Make-Ahead
=====================================

Servings: 6–8 egg bites | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25–30 min

---

### Ingredients

* 6 large eggs
* 1/2 cup cottage cheese
* 1/2 cup shredded cheese *(cheddar, gruyere, or mix)*
* 1/4 cup milk or heavy cream
* Salt and pepper, to taste

---

### Add-In Options (mix it up)

* Cooked bacon, crumbled
* Cooked sausage
* Diced ham
* Different cheeses *(pepper jack, mozzarella, etc.)*

---

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
2. Blend eggs, cottage cheese, milk/cream, salt, and pepper until smooth.
3. Stir in shredded cheese and any add-ins.
4. Pour mixture into greased muffin tin or silicone molds.
5. Place muffin tin in a larger pan and add hot water (water bath).
6. Bake for 25–30 minutes until set.
7. Let cool slightly before removing.

---

### Notes

* Water bath keeps them soft and creamy (key step)
* Store in fridge and reheat for quick breakfasts
* Super customizable depending on what you have
