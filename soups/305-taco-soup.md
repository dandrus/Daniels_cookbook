## 305. Taco Soup

===============================================
Soups   |   Quick Meals   |   Mexican-Inspired
==============================================

Servings: 6–8 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~40 min

---

### Ingredients

* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 small onion, diced
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 packet taco seasoning (or use your homemade recipe)
* 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
* 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
* 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained
* 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
* 1 can (10 oz) Rotel
* 2 cups beef broth

---

### Instructions

1. In a large pot, brown ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess grease.
2. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 3–4 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
4. Stir in taco seasoning.
5. Add beans, corn, tomatoes, Rotel, and broth.
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20–25 minutes.

---

### Optional Toppings

* Shredded cheese
* Sour cream
* Cilantro
* Tortilla chips

---

### Notes

* Uses your taco seasoning recipe (210) if desired
* Easy to scale up
* Even better the next day
