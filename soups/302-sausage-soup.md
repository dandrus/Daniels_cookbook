## Sausage Soup
*I Am Homesteader*

Servings: 6 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~40 min

### Ingredients
- 1 lb Italian sausage (mild or hot)
- 1 Tbsp olive oil (if needed)
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup carrots, sliced
- 1 cup celery, sliced
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- ½ tsp black pepper
- Salt, to taste
- 2 cups baby spinach (optional)
- Grated Parmesan, for serving (optional)

### Instructions
1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it up as it cooks.
2. Remove excess grease if necessary.
3. Add onion and sauté until softened, about 4–5 minutes.
4. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. Stir in chicken broth, diced tomatoes, beans, carrots, celery, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
7. Stir in spinach (if using) and cook 1–2 minutes until wilted.
8. Taste and season with salt as needed.
9. Serve hot with grated Parmesan on top.

### Notes / Tips
- Great with crusty bread or garlic toast.
- Swap cannellini beans for kidney or great northern beans if needed.
- Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for extra heat.
- This soup freezes well for quick lunches.
