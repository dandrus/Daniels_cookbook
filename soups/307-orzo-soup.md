## Sausage Orzo Soup
The Flavours of Kitchen

Servings: 6 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~40 min

Ingredients
- 1 lb Italian sausage (mild or hot), casings removed
- 1 Tbsp olive oil (if needed)
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 6 cups chicken broth
- 3/4 cup uncooked orzo
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 2 cups kale, chopped
- Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

Instructions
1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown sausage over medium heat, breaking it up as it cooks.
2. Remove excess grease if needed.
3. Add onion and cook until softened, about 4–5 minutes.
4. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
6. Stir in orzo and Italian seasoning.
7. Reduce heat and simmer 10–12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until orzo is tender.
8. Stir in kale and cook 3–5 minutes until softened.
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
10. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan if desired.

Notes
- Stir occasionally so the orzo does not stick.
- Soup thickens as it sits; add broth when reheating.
- Kale holds up better than spinach for leftovers.
