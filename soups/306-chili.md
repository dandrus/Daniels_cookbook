## Easy Chili
McCormick

Servings: 6 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~40 min

Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
- 2 Tbsp McCormick Chili Seasoning
- 1/2 cup water
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions
1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown ground beef over medium heat.
2. Add onion and cook until softened, about 4–5 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans, tomato sauce, chili seasoning, and water.
5. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat.
6. Simmer uncovered for 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
8. Serve hot.

Notes
- Easily doubles for crowds or leftovers.
- Add corn or black beans if you like extra texture.
- Even better the next day.
