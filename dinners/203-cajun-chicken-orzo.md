## Cajun Chicken Orzo
*Supergolden Bakes*

Servings: 4 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: ~35 min

### Ingredients
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 Tbsp Cajun seasoning
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 Tbsp butter
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup uncooked orzo pasta
- 2½ cups chicken broth
- ½ cup heavy cream
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt & black pepper, to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

### Instructions
1. Season chicken evenly with Cajun seasoning.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through; remove and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, melt butter and add onion. Cook until softened, about 3–4 minutes.
5. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
6. Stir in orzo and toast lightly for 1 minute.
7. Pour in chicken broth, bring to a simmer, and cook uncovered for 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until orzo is tender.
8. Reduce heat to low; stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until creamy.
9. Return chicken to the skillet and cook 2–3 minutes to heat through.
10. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garnish with parsley if desired.

### Notes / Tips
- Use mild or hot Cajun seasoning depending on heat preference.
- Add spinach at the end for a quick veggie boost.
- If sauce thickens too much, loosen with a splash of broth.
- Excellent one-pan weeknight meal.
