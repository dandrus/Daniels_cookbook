## Spam Musubi Bake
FeedMi

Servings: 6–8 | Prep: 15 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~45 min

Ingredients
- 3 cups cooked white rice
- 2 cans (12 oz each) Spam, diced
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup sriracha (adjust to taste)
- Furikake seasoning, to taste
- Green onions, chopped (optional)

Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a baking dish.
2. Spread cooked rice evenly in the bottom of the dish.
3. Sprinkle furikake seasoning evenly over the rice.
4. In a skillet over medium heat, cook diced Spam until lightly browned.
5. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, and rice vinegar to the Spam.
6. Cook until sauce thickens and coats the Spam.
7. Spread Spam mixture evenly over the rice.
8. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise and sriracha.
9. Drizzle mayo mixture evenly over the top.
10. Bake uncovered for 25–30 minutes, until heated through and lightly golden.
11. Garnish with green onions if desired and serve warm.

Notes
- Serve with roasted seaweed snacks for scooping.
- Adjust sriracha for heat preference.
- Leftovers reheat well.
