## Creamy Chicken Enchiladas
*The Country Cook*

Servings: 6 | Prep: 15 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~45 min

### Ingredients

**Enchiladas**
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie works great)
- 1 can (10 oz) red enchilada sauce
- 1 cup shredded cheddar or Mexican-blend cheese
- 8 flour tortillas (8-inch)

**Creamy Sauce**
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of chicken soup
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp onion powder
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

**Topping**
- ½ cup shredded cheese
- Chopped cilantro or green onions (optional)

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a 9×13 baking dish.
2. In a bowl, mix shredded chicken, enchilada sauce, and 1 cup shredded cheese.
3. Spoon chicken mixture evenly into tortillas, roll up, and place seam-side down in the baking dish.
4. In another bowl, whisk together cream cheese, sour cream, cream of chicken soup, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper until smooth.
5. Pour creamy sauce evenly over enchiladas.
6. Sprinkle remaining cheese over the top.
7. Bake uncovered for 25–30 minutes, until bubbly and lightly golden.
8. Garnish with cilantro or green onions if desired and serve hot.

### Notes / Tips
- Swap red enchilada sauce for green if preferred.
- Corn tortillas work too, but warm them first to prevent cracking.
- Add diced green chiles to the sauce for extra flavor.
- Freezes well before baking; thaw overnight and bake as directed.
