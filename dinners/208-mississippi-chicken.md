## Mississippi Chicken
Salt & Lavender

Servings: 6 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 6–7 hr | Total: ~6 hr

### Ingredients
- 2 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
- 1 packet ranch seasoning mix
- 1 packet brown gravy mix
- ½ cup unsalted butter (1 stick)
- 6–8 pepperoncini peppers
- ¼ cup pepperoncini juice

### Instructions
1. Place chicken in the bottom of a slow cooker.
2. Sprinkle ranch seasoning and brown gravy mix evenly over the chicken.
3. Place butter on top of the chicken.
4. Add pepperoncini peppers and pour in pepperoncini juice.
5. Cover and cook on LOW for 6–7 hours, or until chicken is very tender.
6. Shred chicken with two forks and stir to coat in the sauce.
7. Serve hot.

### Notes / Tips
- Chicken thighs stay juicier, but breasts work fine.
- Serve over mashed potatoes, rice, or on sandwich rolls.
- Add more pepperoncini juice for extra tang.
- Leftovers reheat extremely well.
