## One-Pan Herb Chicken & Vegetable Skillet
Family recipe

Servings: 4 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: ~35 min

Ingredients
- 1.5 lb boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 medium carrots, sliced into coins
- 1 cup green beans, trimmed and halved
- 1 medium zucchini, sliced into half-moons
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp dried thyme (or Italian seasoning)
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions
1. Season chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Sear chicken 5–6 minutes until golden; remove and set aside.
4. In the same pan, sauté onion and carrots 3–4 minutes until starting to soften.
5. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
6. Add green beans and zucchini; stir to combine.
7. Pour in chicken broth and scrape up browned bits from the pan.
8. Return chicken to skillet.
9. Cover and simmer on medium-low 8–10 minutes until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
10. Adjust seasoning and garnish with parsley if desired.
11. Serve as-is or over rice, quinoa, or crusty bread.

Notes
- Thighs stay juicier, but breasts work well.
- Do not overcrowd the pan when searing.
- Great for meal prep.
