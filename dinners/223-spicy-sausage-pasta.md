## Spicy Sausage Pasta

Servings: 6-8 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~35 min

### Ingredients

- 1 lb penne or spaghetti
- 1.5 lbs ground sausage
- 1 jar alfredo sauce
- 1 jar marinara sauce
- 1 can (10 oz) Rotel diced tomatoes and green chiles
- 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste

### Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain and set aside.
2. Brown ground sausage in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat, breaking it up as it cooks.
3. Drain excess grease.
4. Add alfredo sauce, marinara sauce, Rotel, and tomato paste; stir to combine.
5. Heat over medium heat until the sauce begins to bubble.
6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Serve over pasta.

### Notes

- Start the pasta water while browning the sausage so they finish around the same time.
- Mild sausage works well; use hot sausage or add red pepper flakes for extra heat.
