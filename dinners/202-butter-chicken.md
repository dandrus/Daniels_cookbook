## Butter Chicken
*Chili Pepper Madness*

Servings: 4–6 | Prep: 20 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: ~50 min

### Ingredients

**Chicken Marinade**
- 1½ lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into chunks
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 1 Tbsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- Salt, to taste

**Butter Chicken Sauce**
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 Tbsp fresh ginger, minced
- 1 Tbsp garam masala
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- ½–1 tsp chili powder (to taste)
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Salt, to taste

### Instructions
1. In a bowl, combine all marinade ingredients and add chicken; mix well to coat.
2. Cover and marinate at least 30 minutes (up to overnight for best flavor).
3. Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add onion and cook until soft and lightly golden, about 5–7 minutes.
5. Add garlic and ginger; cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
6. Stir in garam masala, coriander, cumin, paprika, and chili powder; toast briefly.
7. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer 10–15 minutes until thickened.
8. Add marinated chicken and cook 10–12 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
9. Stir in heavy cream and simmer 5 minutes.
10. Taste and adjust salt and heat level before serving.

### Notes / Tips
- Chicken thighs stay juicier, but breasts work fine.
- For smoother sauce, blend before adding chicken.
- Serve with basmati rice and naan.
- Leftovers reheat well and deepen in flavor overnight.
