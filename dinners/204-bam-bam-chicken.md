## Chica Licka Bam Bam Baked Chicken Thighs
*Family recipe*

Servings: 4–6 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 1 hr | Total: ~1 hr 5 min

### Ingredients
- 1½–2 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- CHICA LICKA BAM BAM seasoning, to taste

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Place chicken thighs evenly in a 9×13 baking dish.
3. Season generously on all sides with CHICA LICKA BAM BAM.
4. Cover dish tightly with foil.
5. Bake for 45 minutes.
6. Remove foil and return dish to oven.
7. Bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes to brown slightly.
8. Remove from oven and rest 5 minutes before serving.

### Notes / Tips
- Great over rice or mashed potatoes with pan juices.
- Add vegetables (broccoli, green beans, or carrots) during the uncovered bake if desired.
- If chicken releases a lot of liquid, spoon some off before the final 15 minutes for better browning.
- This one scales perfectly for meal prep.
