## American Chop Suey
*XOXO Bella*

Servings: 6 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 35 min | Total: ~45 min

### Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 Tbsp olive oil (if needed)
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
- 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- ½ tsp paprika
- Salt & black pepper, to taste
- 2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
- 2 cups water or beef broth

### Instructions
1. Cook elbow macaroni according to package directions; drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet or pot, brown ground beef over medium heat; drain excess grease.
3. Add onion and bell pepper; cook until softened, about 4–5 minutes.
4. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, Italian seasoning, paprika, salt, and pepper.
6. Simmer sauce for 10–15 minutes to let flavors develop.
7. Add cooked macaroni and stir until fully combined.
8. Simmer 5 minutes more, stirring occasionally.
9. Serve hot.

### Notes / Tips
- This reheats extremely well and tastes better the next day.
- Swap ground beef for ground turkey if desired.
- Add shredded cheddar on top for a Midwest-style upgrade.
- Great freezer meal for busy weeks.
