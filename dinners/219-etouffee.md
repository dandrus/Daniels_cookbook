**Dinner  |  Cajun  |  Seafood**

## Crawfish Étouffée
Cajun Ninja

Servings: 6 | Prep: 20 min | Cook: 45 min | Total: ~1 hr 5 min

Ingredients

Roux
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Vegetables
- 1 medium onion, finely diced
- 1 green bell pepper, finely diced
- 2 celery ribs, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

Etouffee
- 1 lb crawfish tails, thawed
- 3 cups seafood stock or water
- 1 Tbsp Cajun seasoning
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- Salt, to taste
- 2 green onions, chopped

For Serving
- Cooked white rice

Instructions
1. In a heavy-bottomed pot, melt butter over medium heat.
2. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until the roux is light to medium brown.
3. Add onion, bell pepper, and celery to the roux; cook until soft and fragrant.
4. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. Slowly stir in seafood stock until smooth.
6. Add Cajun seasoning, paprika, black pepper, and salt.
7. Simmer 20–25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
8. Add crawfish tails and green onions.
9. Simmer 10–15 minutes, do not boil.
10. Taste and adjust seasoning.
11. Serve hot over white rice.

Notes
- Do not boil after adding crawfish or they will become tough.
- A lighter roux is traditional for etouffee.
- Leftovers reheat gently on low heat.
