## Easy Goulash
*I Wash You Dry*

Servings: 6 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 35 min | Total: ~45 min

### Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 Tbsp olive oil (if needed)
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
- 3 cups beef broth
- 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
- 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1½ Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1½ tsp Italian seasoning
- 1–2 bay leaves (optional)
- Salt & black pepper, to taste
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

### Instructions
1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown ground beef over medium heat; drain excess grease.
2. Add onion and cook until softened, about 4–5 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Stir in beef broth, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, Italian seasoning, bay leaves (if using), salt, and pepper.
5. Bring to a boil, then stir in uncooked macaroni.
6. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook 12–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is tender.
7. Remove bay leaves if used.
8. Stir in shredded cheddar if desired.
9. Serve hot.

### Notes / Tips
- Bay leaves add background flavor but are totally optional.
- Stir occasionally so the pasta doesn’t stick.
- Add a splash of broth if it thickens too much.
- Excellent leftovers and freezes well.
