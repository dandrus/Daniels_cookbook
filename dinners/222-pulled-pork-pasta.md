## Creamy Leftover Pulled Pork Pasta
Whole Made Living

Servings: 4 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min | Total: ~30 min

Ingredients
- 12 oz pasta (penne or rotini)
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup leftover pulled pork
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Red pepper flakes (optional)
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain and set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Add pulled pork and heat through.
5. Stir in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
6. Reduce heat and stir in heavy cream.
7. Add Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and smooth.
8. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using.
9. Add cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
10. Garnish with parsley if desired and serve hot.

Notes
- Works best with sauced pulled pork.
- Add peas or spinach for extra vegetables.
- Loosen sauce with a splash of pasta water if needed.
