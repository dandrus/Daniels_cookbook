## Slow Cooker Salisbury Steak Meatballs
*Together As Family*

Servings: 6 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 5–6 hr | Total: ~6 hr

### Ingredients

**Meatballs & Gravy**
- 1 bag (26 oz) frozen meatballs (about 30–35)
- 2 cups reduced-sodium beef broth
- 1 packet brown gravy mix
- 1 packet onion soup mix
- 2 Tbsp ketchup
- 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

- Mashed pototes or egg noodles

**Cornstarch Slurry**
- 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- 2 Tbsp cold water

### Instructions
1. Spray the slow cooker insert with non-stick cooking spray.
2. Add frozen meatballs directly to the slow cooker.
3. In a bowl, whisk together beef broth, gravy mix, onion soup mix, ketchup, and Worcestershire sauce until smooth.
4. Pour gravy mixture over meatballs and stir gently to coat.
5. Cover and cook on **LOW** for 5–6 hours.
6. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and cold water to form a slurry.
7. Stir slurry into the slow cooker and cook an additional 10 minutes, or until gravy thickens.
8. Serve hot over mashed potatoes or egg noodles.

### Notes / Tips
- No need to thaw meatballs before cooking.
- Reduced-sodium broth helps keep the dish from getting overly salty.
- For thinner gravy, use 1 Tbsp cornstarch instead of 2.
- Pairs especially well with mashed potatoes or buttered egg noodles.
