## Buffalo Chicken Dip
*Frank's RedHot*

Servings: 10–12 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: ~35 min

### Ingredients
- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie works great)
- 8 oz cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup Frank's RedHot sauce (or buffalo sauce of choice)
- ½ cup ranch dressing
- 1½ cups shredded cheddar or Colby Jack cheese, divided
- Green onions, sliced (optional, for garnish)

**For Serving**
- Tortilla chips
- Celery sticks
- Crackers

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a small baking dish or pie plate.
2. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese, buffalo sauce, and ranch dressing until smooth.
3. Stir in shredded chicken and 1 cup of the shredded cheese.
4. Spread mixture evenly into prepared dish.
5. Top with remaining ½ cup of cheese.
6. Bake uncovered 20–25 minutes, until bubbly and lightly golden on top.
7. Garnish with green onions if desired and serve hot with chips, celery, or crackers.

### Notes / Tips
- Can also be made in a slow cooker on LOW for 1–2 hours.
- Blue cheese dressing can be substituted for ranch.
- Add more hot sauce for extra heat.
- Doubles easily for large gatherings.
