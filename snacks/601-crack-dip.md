## Crack Dip
Family recipe

Servings: 8–10 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 2 hr | Total: ~2 hr 10 min

Ingredients
- 16 oz sour cream
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1 (10 oz) can Rotel, undrained
- 1.5 lb ground sausage (Falls Brand spicy sausage preferred)
- Tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions
1. Brown ground sausage in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
2. Drain excess grease if needed.
3. Add cooked sausage, sour cream, cream cheese, and Rotel to a crock pot.
4. Stir to combine.
5. Cook on LOW for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
6. Once hot and fully melted, stir well and keep warm for serving.
7. Serve with scoping tortilla chips.

Notes
- Best made about 2 hours before serving.
- Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
- Can be doubled easily for large gatherings.
- Leftovers reheat well.
