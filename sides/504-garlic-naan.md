## Buttery Garlic Naan
*Cafe Delites*

Servings: 8 naan | Prep: 15 min | Cook: 15 min | Total: ~30 min

### Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp instant yeast
- ½ tsp salt
- ¾ cup warm milk
- 2 Tbsp plain yogurt
- 2 Tbsp olive oil

**Garlic Butter**
- 3 Tbsp butter, melted
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
2. Add warm milk, yogurt, and olive oil; mix until a soft dough forms.
3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead 2–3 minutes until smooth.
4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1 hour or until doubled in size.
5. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces and roll each into an oval about ¼-inch thick.
6. Heat a cast-iron skillet or heavy pan over medium-high heat.
7. Cook naan one at a time for 1–2 minutes per side, until bubbles form and spots are lightly charred.
8. Mix melted butter and garlic; brush generously over hot naan.
9. Sprinkle with parsley if desired and serve warm.

### Notes / Tips
- Best cooked in a hot cast-iron skillet for authentic char.
- Add minced garlic directly to the dough for extra garlic flavor.
- Keeps well refrigerated for 2–3 days; reheat in a dry skillet.
- Excellent with curries, soups, or as a flatbread wrap.
