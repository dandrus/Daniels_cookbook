## Southern Baked Macaroni and Cheese
*The Cozy Cook*

Servings: 8–10 | Prep: 15 min | Cook: 35 min | Total: ~50 min

### Ingredients

**Pasta**
- 1 lb elbow macaroni

**Cheese Sauce**
- ½ cup unsalted butter
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 3 cups whole milk
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp dry mustard powder
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

**Cheese Blend**
- 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 cup Gruyère or Monterey Jack, shredded
- 1 cup Colby Jack, shredded

**Topping**
- 1 cup sharp cheddar, shredded
- ½ cup panko breadcrumbs (optional)
- 1 Tbsp butter, melted (for breadcrumbs)

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9×13 baking dish.
2. Cook macaroni according to package directions until just al dente; drain and set aside.
3. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook 1–2 minutes.
4. Gradually whisk in milk and heavy cream until smooth.
5. Cook, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens, about 5–7 minutes.
6. Stir in garlic powder, onion powder, mustard powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
7. Remove from heat and stir in cheese blend until fully melted and smooth.
8. Add cooked macaroni and stir to coat evenly.
9. Pour into prepared baking dish.
10. Top with remaining cheddar.
11. If using breadcrumbs, toss with melted butter and sprinkle over the top.
12. Bake uncovered 25–30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
13. Rest 5 minutes before serving.

### Notes / Tips
- Shred your own cheese — pre-shredded cheese has coatings that prevent smooth melting.
- Don't overcook the pasta; it continues to cook in the oven.
- Add a layer of cheese in the middle of the casserole for extra richness.
- Excellent as a holiday side or potluck dish.
