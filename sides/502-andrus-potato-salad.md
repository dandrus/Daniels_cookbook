## Family Potato Salad

Servings: ~6–8 | Prep: 15 min | Cook: 15–20 min

### Ingredients

* 6 large russet potatoes, peeled and diced
* 5 eggs
* ~1/2 cup mayo *(start with less and add more as needed)*
* ~1 Tbsp mustard *(adjust to taste)*
* Tony's seasoning *(generous amount)*
* Garlic powder, to taste
* Salt and pepper, to taste

### Instructions

1. Peel and dice potatoes into chunks.
2. Add potatoes and eggs to a pot and cover with water.
3. Bring to a boil and cook until potatoes are soft.
4. Drain water and peel the eggs.
5. In a large bowl, combine potatoes and eggs and mash together.
6. Add mayo and mustard, then mix.
7. Add Tony's seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
8. Mix and taste.
9. Adjust mayo and mustard until creamy and flavor is right.

### Notes

* Don't be shy with Tony's — this recipe leans on it
* Adjust mayo/mustard gradually until texture is right
* Best made ahead — flavor improves after sitting in the fridge
