## French Silk Chocolate Pie
*Pillsbury*

Servings: 8 | Prep: 20 min | Chill: 2 hr | Total: ~2 hr 20 min

### Ingredients

**Crust**
- 1 refrigerated chocolate cookie pie crust (9-inch), baked and cooled

**Filling**
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 2 oz unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream

**Topping**
- Sweetened whipped cream
- Chocolate shavings or curls (optional)

### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, beat sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
2. Add melted chocolate and vanilla; beat until well combined.
3. Add eggs one at a time, beating on high speed for 5 minutes after each egg.
4. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
5. Gently fold whipped cream into chocolate mixture.
6. Spoon filling into cooled pie crust and smooth the top.
7. Refrigerate at least 2 hours until set.
8. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings before serving.

### Notes / Tips
- Beat the eggs the full 5 minutes each for proper texture.
- For food safety, use pasteurized eggs if preferred.
- Best served well chilled.
- Classic holiday and special-occasion dessert.
