## Southern Banana Pudding
*Paula Deen*

Servings: 10–12 | Prep: 20 min | Chill: 2 hr | Total: ~2 hr 20 min

### Ingredients
- 1 box (5.1 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
- 3 cups cold whole milk
- 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
- 8 oz cream cheese, softened
- 12 oz whipped topping (Cool Whip), divided
- 1 box (11 oz) Nilla Wafers
- 4–5 ripe bananas, sliced

### Instructions
1. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth.
2. Add sweetened condensed milk and mix until well combined.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk instant pudding mix with cold milk for 2 minutes until thickened.
4. Fold pudding into the cream cheese mixture.
5. Fold in half of the whipped topping until smooth.
6. In a large trifle dish or 9×13 pan, layer in order: Nilla Wafers, banana slices, pudding mixture.
7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with pudding on top.
8. Spread remaining whipped topping over the top.
9. Crush a few extra Nilla Wafers and sprinkle on top for garnish.
10. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

### Notes / Tips
- The wafers soften into a cake-like texture after chilling overnight — this is the goal.
- Slice bananas just before layering to prevent browning.
- Best made the day before serving.
- Keeps refrigerated up to 3 days.
