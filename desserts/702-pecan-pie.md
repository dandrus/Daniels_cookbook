## Homemade Pecan Pie
Karo Syrup

Servings: 8 | Prep: 15 min | Bake: 50–60 min | Total: ~1 hr 15 min

### Ingredients
- 1 unbaked 9-inch pie crust
- 1 cup Karo light corn syrup
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 2 Tbsp butter, melted
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1½ cups pecan halves

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Place unbaked pie crust into a 9-inch pie plate.
3. In a large bowl, whisk together corn syrup, sugar, eggs, melted butter, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
4. Stir in pecan halves.
5. Pour filling into the pie crust.
6. Bake for 50–60 minutes, until filling is set and center is slightly firm.
7. Cool completely on a wire rack before slicing.

### Notes / Tips
- Cover edges of the crust with foil if browning too quickly.
- Pie firms up as it cools — don’t rush slicing.
- Serve plain or with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
- Keeps well refrigerated for several days.
